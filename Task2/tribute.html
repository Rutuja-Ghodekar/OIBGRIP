Neerja Bhanot

India's Youngest BraveHeart

"Mar jaungi Lekin ,Bhaagungi Nahin"

Neerja Bhanot a flight attendant born on September 7,1963 is remembered for here selfless act as the head purser who died while saving passengers.She sacrificed her life on September 5,1986,while shielding three children from gunshot on the hijacked Pan Am Flight 73.She is honored by the award ASHOKA CHAKRA .She has inspired millions of girls and youth by her bravery. She truely will be remebered by our whole nation even after centuries. INDIA is blessed to have Neerja Bhanot ,a girl with lots of courage and brave heart.
